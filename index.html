Music Producer/Songwriter/Composer

mask1 On Da Track

Producing music for over 30 years. Yes, he started when they had Cassette Tapes. And still making a great living off of his passion... Multi-genre is the accurate word for what he does. There's no box that can contain his creativity. But started with mixtapes and hip-hop.
Born in Nashville, Tennessee before moving to Atlanta @ the age of 5. Then traveling all over the U.S. staying in cities like Charleston, SC; Columbus, OH and Albany, GA. Naming a few places he lived and considered home. Call him multi-cultural, if you will. Before returning to his birthplace to reunite with his family.
Some that know him say that his story is a story that should be in a book or a movie. Yet he continues to be modest and humble it seems. He rarely speaks about his accolades or accomplishments over talking about others deeds and great gifts. Saying that he does not want any of his prior collabs or placements with artist listed. He says that he stands on his craft alone. He does not want to work with people based on whom he worked with before. Though he values his relationship with others, his network and having a team.
He also thinks that his team is, and should always be first. Loyalty over BS... Family Over All.
